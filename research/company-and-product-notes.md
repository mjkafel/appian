# Company And Product Notes

## One-Line Understanding

Appian provides AI-powered process automation for complex enterprise and government workflows. Its platform brings together process automation, low-code application development, data fabric, AI, case management, integrations, and customer implementation services.

## Product Notes

From Appian's official site:

- Appian describes its platform as AI automation for critical processes and emphasizes orchestrating AI agents, systems, and people from one platform.
- The platform includes AI, low-code development, data fabric, process automation, API integration, business process management, process intelligence, and case management.
- Appian's AI positioning is process-centered. The site emphasizes that AI works better when embedded into business processes with structure, data, guardrails, analytics, and scalability.
- Appian Data Fabric securely connects disparate systems into a single ontology for real-time operations and enterprise AI agents. Appian describes data fabric as a way to unify data without migration and empower users and AI agents to read, write, and update information across source systems.
- Appian API Integration supports reusable integrations, prebuilt connectors, OpenAPI, REST/SOAP-style integration patterns, JDBC/database connectivity, and the Integration SDK.
- Appian Customer Success implementation messaging emphasizes end-to-end fulfillment, speed to value, and customer empowerment.

## Why It Matters For Your Interview

The Senior Consultant role is not just "consulting" and not just "coding." It sits in the middle:

- Understand client business processes.
- Build custom applications on Appian.
- Model relational data.
- Integrate multiple enterprise systems.
- Apply AI tools such as RAG and agentic workflows.
- Test and deliver in Agile teams.
- Enable customers to become self-sufficient.
