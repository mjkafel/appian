## Core Positioning

You are a customer-facing technical consultant with strong process modernization, requirements-to-handoff ownership, data mapping, API/integration thinking, SQL/Python exposure, AWS/cloud-native awareness, and AI-assisted analysis experience.

The strongest Appian story is:

> I have not built production Appian applications yet, but the work Appian Customer Success does is very aligned with my background: understand a client's business process, model the workflow and data, define integrations, build or guide implementation, test the solution, and enable the customer to operate independently.

## Interview Frame

Lead with current Booz Allen solutioning and modernization work. Then use iCIMS to prove SaaS implementation and integrations. Use Oracle to prove technical consulting, cloud-native deployment awareness, observability, and customer knowledge transfer.

Keep the tone honest and confident:

- Do not pretend to be an Appian developer already.
- Do not make yourself sound like only a requirements analyst.
- Emphasize that you are technical enough to build/ramp, but strongest where process, data, integration, and client delivery meet.
